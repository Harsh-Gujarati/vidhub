About PromptLunarX

Our Mission

PromptLunarX is dedicated to showcasing the incredible creativity of the AI art community. We provide a curated platform where users can discover, explore, and enjoy the most popular AI-generated videos and images from Civitai's vibrant creative ecosystem.

What We Do

We aggregate and present AI-generated content in an intuitive, visually appealing interface. Our platform makes it easy to:

  • Browse trending AI-generated videos and images
  • Discover new creators and their work
  • Save and organize your favorite content
  • Stay updated with the latest AI art trends

Content Source

All content displayed on PromptLunarX is sourced from the Civitai API. Civitai is a leading platform for AI art generation and sharing. We are not affiliated with Civitai but serve as an independent aggregation and discovery platform for their publicly available content.
